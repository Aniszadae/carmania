BMW M4

Anis Zafar      22/05/2024 19:30      120

The BMW M4 is a high-performance sports car that blends aggressive styling, advanced technology, and exhilarating driving dynamics. It features a 3.0L twin-turbocharged inline-six engine, delivering 473 horsepower in the standard model and up to 503 horsepower in the Competition model. Renowned for its precise handling and luxurious interior, the M4 is a top choice for driving enthusiasts.

Price: $74,700

History and Evolution

2014-2020 (First Generation - F82)

The BMW M4 was introduced as a successor to the BMW M3 Coupe, featuring a 3.0L twin-turbo inline-six engine. It gained acclaim for its sharp handling, powerful performance, and advanced technology, including adaptive suspension and high-performance braking systems.

2021-Present (Second Generation - G82)

The current generation M4 builds on the success of its predecessor with even more power and advanced features. The G82 offers a bold new design, enhanced aerodynamics, and cutting-edge technology. It is available in both standard and Competition models, with the latter offering additional horsepower and performance upgrades.

Specifications:

Specification Details
Engine 3.0L twin-turbocharged inline-six
Horsepower 473 hp (standard), 503 hp (Competition)
Transmission 6-speed manual or 8-speed automatic
Fuel Economy 16 mpg city / 23 mpg highway
Seating Capacity 4
Infotainment 10.25-inch touchscreen, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto
Safety Features Active Driving Assistant, Blind Spot Detection, Lane Departure Warning, Front Collision Warning
Additional Highlights The M4 also offers a range of performance features such as M Servotronic steering, adaptive M suspension, and M Compound brakes. Optional features include carbon-fiber interior trim, M Carbon bucket seats, and a high-performance M Driver's Package.
