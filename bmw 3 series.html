BMW 3 Series

Anis Zafar      6/03/2024 14:00      85

The BMW 3 Series is a compact executive car known for its sporty performance, luxurious interior, and advanced technology. It features a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine, delivering 255 horsepower. With dynamic handling, premium materials, and cutting-edge infotainment, the 3 Series remains a top choice for driving enthusiasts and professionals alike.

Price: $41,000

History and Evolution

1975-1983 (First Generation - E21)

The BMW 3 Series was introduced as a two-door sedan, setting a new standard for compact executive cars with its sporty driving dynamics and premium feel. The E21 was celebrated for its precision engineering and driving pleasure.

1982-1994 (Second Generation - E30)

The E30 expanded the 3 Series lineup with four-door sedans, wagons, and the iconic M3. This generation solidified the 3 Series’ reputation for performance and introduced new technology, such as electronic fuel injection.

1990-2000 (Third Generation - E36)

The E36 featured a more aerodynamic design, improved handling, and advanced safety features. It was the first 3 Series to offer a hatchback (the Compact) and was praised for its balanced performance and luxury.

1998-2006 (Fourth Generation - E46)

The E46 brought further refinements in design, quality, and performance. It introduced new technologies, including navigation systems and more advanced stability control. The E46 M3 is especially revered for its high-revving inline-six engine and precision handling.

2005-2013 (Fifth Generation - E90/E91/E92/E93)

This generation included the sedan (E90), wagon (E91), coupe (E92), and convertible (E93). The E90 series enhanced luxury and performance, with turbocharged engines and a focus on driver-centric features.

2011-2019 (Sixth Generation - F30/F31/F34)

The F30/F31/F34 series offered new levels of efficiency and technology, with turbocharged engines across the range and advanced infotainment systems. The introduction of the Gran Turismo (F34) added versatility to the lineup.

2018-Present (Seventh Generation - G20/G21)

The current G20/G21 generation of the 3 Series features a bold, modern design with enhanced aerodynamics and cutting-edge technology. It offers improved performance with a range of efficient and powerful engines, including plug-in hybrid options. The interior boasts luxurious materials, a high-tech digital cockpit, and advanced driver-assistance systems.

Specifications:

  • Engine: 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder
  • Horsepower: 255 hp
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic
  • Fuel Economy: 26 mpg city / 36 mpg highway
  • Seating Capacity: 5
  • Infotainment: 10.25-inch touchscreen, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto
  • Safety Features: BMW Active Driving Assistant, Blind Spot Detection, Lane Departure Warning, Adaptive Cruise Control
  • Additional Highlights: The 3 Series also offers an M Sport package for enhanced performance and styling, as well as a plug-in hybrid model (330e) that combines efficiency with BMW's signature driving dynamics.
