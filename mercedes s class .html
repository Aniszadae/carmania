Mercedes-Benz G-Class

Michael Lee      8/08/2024 16:00      100

The Mercedes-Benz G-Class is an iconic luxury SUV renowned for its distinctive boxy design, robust off-road capability, and opulent interior. It features a 4.0L V8 biturbo engine, delivering 416 horsepower. With its blend of rugged durability and sophisticated amenities, the G-Class is a symbol of status and performance.

Price: $131,750

History and Evolution

1979-1991 (First Generation - W460)

The G-Class was originally developed as a military vehicle and quickly gained a civilian following for its ruggedness and reliability. The W460 featured a spartan interior and was available in various body styles, including two-door and four-door variants.

1990-2018 (Second Generation - W463)

The W463 brought significant upgrades in luxury and technology. It maintained the classic G-Class silhouette while introducing more powerful engines, advanced off-road capabilities, and a luxurious interior. The G 500 and AMG versions became particularly popular.

2018-Present (Third Generation - W464)

The current generation G-Class offers modern enhancements while preserving its iconic design. It features a new independent front suspension for improved on-road handling, a more spacious interior, and the latest in Mercedes-Benz technology, including the MBUX infotainment system.

Specifications:

Specification Details
Engine 4.0L V8 biturbo
Horsepower 416 hp
Transmission 9-speed automatic
Fuel Economy 13 mpg city / 17 mpg highway
Seating Capacity 5
Infotainment 12.3-inch touchscreen, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto
Safety Features Active Distance Assist DISTRONIC, Blind Spot Assist, Lane Keeping Assist, Active Brake Assist
Additional Highlights The G-Class also features advanced off-road capabilities with three locking differentials, a luxurious interior with Nappa leather upholstery, and optional AMG performance packages for enhanced driving dynamics.
